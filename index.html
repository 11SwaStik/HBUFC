About the Club

Riverside FC is a community football club founded in 1924. We bring together players of all ages and abilities, united by a love for the beautiful game.

Our Mission

To grow the game in our community, develop local talent, and bring people together through football.

Our Values

Respect, teamwork, and inclusion — both on and off the pitch. Everyone is welcome at Riverside.

Our Home

Riverside Park Stadium has been our home for nearly a century, where memories are made every matchday.
