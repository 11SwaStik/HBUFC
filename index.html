Our Story

Born from those who were overlooked.

In 2017, in the lanes of Ajmer, talent was everywhere — but opportunity wasn't.

Favouritism and closed circles decided who played and who watched. Gifted footballers were turned away, not for a lack of skill, but for a lack of connections.

HBUFC was built to break that wall. One belief held it up:

Everyone deserves an opportunity to play football.

No gatekeepers. No favourites. Just the game, open to all.
